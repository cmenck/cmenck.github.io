AENG 110 Video Project

I went about making this video project by first assessing what the guidlines were and the task that needed to be completed. The requirements said to make a video promoting something at Millersville. I decided to make my project in support of the Millersville Track and Field Team. I story boarded what I wanted the video to look like and then came up with a main question that I was planning on asking fve of the T&F Athletes. I then went to all of their practices and lift and got the footage that I needed to create my project. I Filmed this project on a Canon T6i with a 70-200mm lense and edited it with Final Cut Pro. I used copyright free music.
